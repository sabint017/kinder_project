Services

"Our best services for your kids"

Smart Attendance

Attendance of students is taken online by the class teacher and is updated in real-time to the parents. Total number of absent days is counted. Once clicking on View Details Teacher can see total number of present and absent days of individual student with date of absent days. Undo button allows teacher to cancel or reverse the last command executed.

Digital Monitoring

With just an access to the internet, parents can digitally monitor their kids via Kinder. Through News Feed parents can see their children activities/progress. Information regarding important notices like holidays, exam schedule, result publication, class performance etc can be viewed on NOTICES Board. Program or any upcoming events can be viewed on EVENTS Board.

Real Time Update

Parents will be updated in real time about their kids with the information provided by the teachers. Updates like registering new children, creating new posts, editing class/food routine can be easily done by teachers. Teacher can posts photos by clicking on Create Post by which parents can see their activities.

Results

There’s a result section via which the parents can get the progress report of their kids so that they don’t have to bother going to the school to see the results. Parents can only see their respective children's result. A bar chart demonstrating average marks on each subject of all students of class helps in overall comparison on each subject. Once marks of all subjects are entered it automatically calculates respective Grades and display Remarks accordingly.
