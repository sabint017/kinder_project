Smart Attendance


Attendance of students is taken online by the class teacher and is updated in real-time to the parents. Total number of absent days is counted.

Digital Monitoring


With just an access to the internet, parents can digitally monitor their kids via Kinder. Through News Feed parents can see their childrens activities/progress.

Real Time Update


Parents will be updated in real time about their kids with the information provided by the teachers. Information regarding important things/events can be viewed on Notice Board.

Result


There’s a result section via which the parents can get the progress report of their kids so that they don’t have to bother going to the school to see the results. Parents can only see their respective childrens result.
